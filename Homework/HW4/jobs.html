Work at JavaJam

Barista

As a Barista, you'll create delicious coffee drinks while ensuring that customers feel welcomed and taken care of.

  • Prepare coffee, espresso, and tea drinks to order.
  • Assist customers with their selections.
  • Maintain a clean coffee bar.
  • Handle cash register and process payments.

Hourly Wage: $15.00/hr

Café Server

As a Café Server, you’ll serve food and beverages, interact with customers, and help maintain a positive atmosphere.

  • Take customer orders and deliver food and beverages to tables.
  • Provide menu suggestions and answer questions.
  • Assist in setting up and cleaning tables.
  • Coordinate with kitchen staff to ensure timely service.

Hourly Wage: $14.00/hr
